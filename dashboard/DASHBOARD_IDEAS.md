# Dashboard Ideas - Future Enhancements

This document contains ideas for future dashboard enhancements. These are not implemented but are tracked here for future reference.

## Product Catalog
- Categories with hierarchical organization
- Tags system for better filtering
- Product bundles (sell multiple products together)
- Product variants (different SKUs for same product)
- Price history tracking
- Scheduled discounts with auto-activation

## Stock & Operations
- Low stock alerts with email notifications
- Supplier restock queue
- Import/Export center with CSV validation
- Inventory audits (compare physical vs digital stock)
- Batch expiration tracking and alerts
- Reorder suggestions based on sales velocity
- Stock movement ledger (full audit trail)

## Orders
- Refund processing
- Partial fulfillment support
- Manual order adjustments
- Customer notes field
- Internal staff comments on orders
- SLA timers (time from order to fulfillment)
- Delivery failure reasons tracking

## Customers
- Customer list with order history
- Customer LTV (lifetime value) calculation
- Fraud flags and risk scoring
- Support ticket integration
- Customer communication history

## Analytics
- Customer cohorts (acquisition by month/quarter)
- Sales funnel analysis
- Top performing coupons
- Staff performance metrics
- Delivery failure reasons breakdown
- Revenue by platform
- Churn analysis

## Security & Operations
- Advanced audit log filters (date range, user, action)
- Two-factor authentication (2FA)
- Session management (view active sessions)
- IP allowlist for admin access
- Webhook logs for integrations
- API key management

## Automation
- Fulfillment rules (auto-delivery conditions)
- Notification system (email, Slack, Discord)
- Low stock alerts
- Abandoned checkout recovery
- Price change notifications

## Reporting
- Daily/weekly/monthly reports via email
- Custom report builder
- Export to Excel/PDF
- Multi-currency reporting
- Tax reporting

## UI/UX
- Dark mode support
- Customizable dashboard layout
- Keyboard shortcuts
- Bulk actions on tables
- Advanced filters on all list pages
- Saved filter presets
